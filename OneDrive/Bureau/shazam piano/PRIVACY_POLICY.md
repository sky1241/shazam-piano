# Privacy Policy for ShazaPiano

**Effective Date**: November 24, 2025  
**Last Updated**: November 24, 2025

ShazaPiano ("we", "our", or "us") operates the ShazaPiano mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Audio Recordings

- **What**: Piano audio recordings you create
- **Purpose**: Generate piano tutorial videos
- **Storage**: Temporarily stored on our servers
- **Retention**: Deleted after 24 hours automatically
- **Sharing**: Never shared with third parties

### 1.2 Generated Videos

- **What**: Piano keyboard videos we generate
- **Storage**: Temporarily on servers, optionally on your device
- **Retention**: Server copies deleted after 7 days
- **Sharing**: Only if you explicitly share them

### 1.3 Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data via Firebase Analytics:
- App opens and sessions
- Features used (recording, practice mode, etc.)
- Purchase events (anonymous)
- Crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)
- Device type and OS version

**We DO NOT collect**:
- Your name or email
- Location data
- Contacts
- Photos or other media
- Microphone access outside recording

### 1.4 Purchase Information

- **What**: In-app purchase verification
- **Purpose**: Unlock premium features
- **Handled by**: Google Play Billing
- **We store**: Only unlock status (boolean), not payment details

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Server Storage

- All stored locally on your device

### 3.3 Security Measures

- Industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure Firebase authentication
- Regular security audits
- Automatic file deletion (retention policies)
- No permanent storage of sensitive data
